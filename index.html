My name is Jake and I'm a web developer, skier, mountain-biker, adventure-taker, outdoorsman. I have a propensity for clean, well-working code as well as elegant, user friendly design. A finished product that marries these ideals is what I strive for.

Currently I am happily employed at Fluid Advertising in Bountiful, UT but am always open to taking on freelance projects.
