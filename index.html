My name is Jake and I'm a web developer, skier, mountain-biker, adventure-taker and outdoors-man. I have a propensity for clean, well-working code as well as elegant, user-friendly design. I strive for a finished product that marries these ideals.

I am always open to taking on freelance projects, so feel free to reach out!
