About Me.

My name is Jake and I'm a web developer, skier, mountain-biker, adventure-taker, outdoorsman. That just about sums it up. I have a propensity for clean, well-working code as well as elegant, user friendly design. A finished product that marries these ideals is what I strive for.

I live in Utah but call Vermont my true home. In the summer, I look forward to the winter. In the winter (after a few heavy storms and a satisfactory number of powder days) I can't help but look forward to the summer. So it goes.

Currently I am happily employed at Fluid Advertising in Bountiful, UT but am always open to taking on freelance projects.
